🌍 How We Clean Your Data

Seismic signals often come with unwanted noise. Our system carefully processes these signals so only the important ground motion remains visible.

  • 📡 Collect: Raw seismic waveforms from trusted networks like IRIS
  • ⚙️ Preprocess: Remove offsets, drifts, and taper edges
  • 🎚️ Filter: Apply band-pass filters to keep useful frequencies
  • 🧭 Analyze: Use polarization to suppress random noise
  • 📊 Visualize: See the difference between raw and cleaned signals

✨ The outcome: clean, reliable seismic data ready for analysis.
